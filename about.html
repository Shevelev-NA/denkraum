About Denkraum

Denkraum is a personal project focused on simple and practical tools for understanding language through text, speech, and media.

The goal is clarity and usefulness — minimal interface, no unnecessary features, and a focus on real language usage.

The project is currently free to use. Optional paid features may be added later, while the core functionality will remain accessible.
